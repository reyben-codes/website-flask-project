MediCare | Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 28, 2025

1. Introduction

At RHU Pila, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how MediCare collects, uses, stores, and protects personal and system-related information from its authorized users (Admins and Staff).

2. Information We Collect

When you register or use MediCare, the system collects the following information:

  • Full name (First, Middle, and Last Name)
  • Username and Password
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Home Address
  • Role within the organization (Admin or Staff)

The system also stores medicine-related data, including generic names, brands, types, variants, and quantities.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To manage and track medicine inventory accurately.
  • To authenticate users and maintain account security.
  • To ensure accountability and record-keeping within Pila's Rural Health Unit (RHU).
  • To improve administrative operations and reporting efficiency.

4. Data Protection and Security

All user data is stored in a secured internal database. Only authorized personnel have access to user information and system records. The system implements authentication and access controls to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse of data.

5. Data Sharing

MediCare does not sell, rent, or share personal data with any external organization. User information is used strictly within Pila's Rural Health Unit (RHU) for official purposes only.

6. Data Retention

Data will be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the operational requirements of RHU Pila or until the account is deactivated by an administrator.

7. User Rights

  • Users may request to update or correct their account information.
  • Users may request account removal by contacting the system administrator.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to comply with new data protection guidelines or system improvements. Users will be notified of significant changes when applicable.

9. Contact

For privacy-related concerns or requests, please contact Pila's Rural Health Unit (RHU) system administrator.

By creating an account and using MediCare, you acknowledge and consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

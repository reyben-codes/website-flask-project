About Us

Our Commitment to Healthcare

We, the health professionals of the Rural Health Unit, commit ourselves to the delivery of accessible, equitable and quality primary health care to the community we serve. Guided by compassion and professionalism, we pledge to provide comprehensive services that include preventive, promotive, curative and rehabilitative care. We strive to protect and uphold the health rights of every individual, ensuring that no one is left behind regardless of social or economic status.

In partnership with local government and community stakeholders, we aim to foster trust, collaboration and empowerment in addressing health challenges. With dedication to continuous improvement, we uphold our responsibility to promote wellness, prevent disease and respond effectively to the needs of our people, thereby building a healthier, more resilient community.

Our Mission

Improvement of the quality of life through effective, responsive, efficient and quality health care delivery system for the people of Pila.
